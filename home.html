About Us

Stuc Coffee Corner, at Panjab University in Chandigarh, are one of the favourite hangout for coffee and conversations. We strive to provide the best experience to our guests. Our objective is to provide an authentic coffee drinking experience in a warm, friendly, no pressure environment. We try to bring relaxation to the city keeping into mind a cozy place that serves coffee,with couches to lounge in while you sip.
